# stealth_audio_recorder.shortcut
IOS Shortcut for covertly recording audio on your IPhone

A few weeks ago, my shit-for-brains psycho neighbor first tried to run me over with his car and then decided to verbally insult me in the harshest way possible. During this event, I was wearing bluetooth headphones and I had no easy way of disabling the headphones and start a recording of the lovely dispute for using it in court.

This IOS shortcut solves the problem for potential future occurrences. Tapping the shortcut will stop all audio playback and disable bluetooth. After dimming the phone's display, a recording will be started. Tapping the phone's display again will sttop the recording, re-enable bluetooth and set the phone's brightness level to 50%. The final recording can then be found in the ICloud's "Shortcuts" folder.
